## Description
SideQuest transforms your bucket list into a social game. Create quests, complete them with photo proof, earn XP, unlock badges, and share your adventures with friends.

**Create & Discover Quests**
Build custom quests with our modular block system — add locations, time limits, difficulty levels, and creative challenges. Or explore thousands of community quests across 8 categories.

**Prove It**
Snap a photo or record a video to prove you completed your quest. Share your proof as a polished share card to Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok, and more.

**Level Up**
Earn XP for every completion. Climb through 5 tiers from Novice to Legend. Maintain weekly streaks for bonus rewards. Unlock 15+ badges that showcase your achievements.

**Challenge Friends**
Send quests to friends and earn bonus XP when they complete them. React to friends' completions in your activity feed. Compete on weekly leaderboards.

**8 Quest Categories**
Travel & Adventure, Food & Drink, Fitness & Sports, Creative & Arts, Social & Community, Career & Learning, Thrill & Adrenaline, Random / Wildcard

**6 Intent Tags**
Growth, Connection, Fun, Challenge, Explore, Create — discover what kind of quester you are.

**SideQuest Pro**
Go ad-free, unlock video proof, get premium share templates, and earn 3 streak freezes per month.

What have you always wanted to do? Stop scrolling. Start doing. Start your SideQuest today.
